About


Several years ago, pre-marriage and when I was still dating via online sites, a friend and I took a roughly 6 mile hike together. As I hadn't seen this friend in a while, I was excited to catch up and hear about any new developments in her life.

However, while returning home, I realized that despite having families, friends, "fur babies" and careers, we'd spent our entire hike discussing nothing but our failed attempts to meet "the one" via online dating. Of course, this drove home just how frustrated we both were as we seemed to face dating disappointment after disappointment, but it also made me curious. What were we doing wrong? Why were we attracting the people we were? And why did it never seem to go anywhere?

These questions led to more questions, and in a search for answers, more reading about the subject of online dating, and what not only sparks but creates lasting relationships and marriages. This research promoted a change in my approach and, soon enough, I met the man of my dreams and now husband.

Now, as both a Couples Counselor and Dating Coach, I help my clients find their own happiness. For while I firmly believe that we should all be flexible, I also believe that we should also never settle or give up on love!
